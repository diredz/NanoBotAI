# 🐈 NanoBotAI

> **Your Private, Local, and Automated Daily Assistant.**

NanoBotAI is a lightweight, fully local AI ecosystem built for macOS M-series. It combines the power of local Large Language Models (LLMs) with automated workflows to deliver news digests, job search results, and interactive AI assistance directly to your Telegram.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

- **🧠 Fully Local Intelligence**: Powered by **Ollama**, running models like Qwen 2.5 and Llama 3.1 entirely on your machine.
- **📱 Telegram Integration**: Chat with your local AI and receive automated updates on the go.
- **🌍 Automated News Digests**: Geopolitical, Entertainment, and AI news fetched and summarized every few hours.
- **💼 Job Search Automation**: Native integration with the German Federal Employment Agency (Arbeitsagentur) for automated daily job alerts.
- **⚡ macOS Native**: Uses `launchd` for efficient, native scheduling without needing third-party background managers.

---

## 🧐 Interesting Facts about NanoBotAI

- **🏠 100% Data Sovereignty**: Unlike commercial AI assistants, your queries and data NEVER leave your machine. It’s AI that respects your privacy by default.
- **💸 Zero Subscription Fees**: By utilizing free public APIs and open-weights models, NanoBotAI provides "Pro" level automation without monthly costs.
- **🍎 Apple Silicon Optimized**: NanoBotAI is tuned to leverage the unified memory and Neural Engine of macOS M-series chips for lightning-fast local inference.
- **🛡️ "Smart" Filtering**: The news engine doesn't just scrape; it categorizes stories into *Breaking*, *Viral*, *Notable*, and *Unusual* based on intelligent headline analysis.
- **🤖 API-First Job Search**: It uses the same API that powers official German job boards, ensuring you get the most accurate and up-to-date listings for specialized "Test Engineer" roles.
